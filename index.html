Frequently Asked Questions

What types of yoga classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of yoga classes including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Yin, and Restorative yoga. Each class is designed to cater to different levels of experience, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Do I need to bring my own yoga mat?

While we encourage you to bring your own mat for personal comfort and hygiene, we also provide mats and other necessary equipment for use during the class at no extra charge.

Are there any membership options available?

Yes, we offer several membership plans including drop-in classes, monthly unlimited passes, and annual memberships to suit your needs and schedule.

Can beginners join your classes?

Absolutely! Our classes are designed to accommodate all skill levels, and our instructors are skilled at modifying poses and providing guidance to beginners.

How can I book a class?

You can book a class through our online scheduling system on our website. Simply select the class you are interested in and follow the prompts to reserve your spot.
